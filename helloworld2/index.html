Drink of the Week: Tokyo Tea

Ingredients Steps
  • 1/2 ounce gin
  • 1/2 ounce melon liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce rum
  • 1/2 ounce tequila
  • 1/2 ounce triple sec
  • 1/2 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce sweet and sour mix*
  • 1 splash club soda
  • Garnish: lemon wheel
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry
  1. Add gin, melon liqueur, rum, tequila, triple sec, vodka and sweet-and-sour mix into a highball glass with ice and stir to combine.
  2. Top with the club soda.
  3. Garnish with a lemon wheel and maraschino cherry.
